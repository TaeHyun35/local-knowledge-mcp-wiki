## Notes

The EU AI Act introduces risk-based AI regulation, including obligations related to high-risk AI systems, transparency, documentation, human oversight, and monitoring. This source is used to connect governance controls to regulatory expectations.

## Candidate Concepts

- Risk classification
- High-risk AI system
- Transparency obligation
- Human oversight
- Technical documentation
- Post-market monitoring
